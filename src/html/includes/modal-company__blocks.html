Helping communities rebuild after natural disaster.

We created Homebound because residential construction is costly, inefficient, and opaque, which results in a universally terrible experience. Homebound is a tech-enabled construction company, starting by rebuilding homes lost in natural disasters with a better labor model and a delightful homeowner experience. In just its first year, Homebound grew to over $200M in presales.
